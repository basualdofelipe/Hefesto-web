<objective>
Refactor the product table from a flat type-only grouping into a 3-level collapsible hierarchy (Type > Name > Finish) and rescope the BOM group editor from type-level to name-level.

Purpose: The business hierarchy is type > name > finish > color/size, but the current UI only groups by type. Users with many products (e.g., 20 "Cinturones" of different names) cannot navigate efficiently. The BOM group editor currently applies to ALL products of a type when the correct grouping is by name — "Hercules" products share a BOM regardless of type.

Output: Hierarchical product tree with collapsible sections at each level, BOM group editor scoped to product name, visual indicators for BOM overrides.
</objective>
